English

*This page is intended for foreigners who speak little English or English learners*

Origin country: Britain/UK

About 380 million people speak English!

Common words and phrases:

- I'm learning English (phrase that tells people English isn't your first language)

- I don't understand (use when you don't understand something)

- Can you please repeat that? (use when you want someone to repeat what they said)

- Can you please talk slower? (use when you want someone to talk slower)

- Hi or hello (use for greetings)

- Please (use when you need help or something done)

- Pardon, Sorry & Excuse Me (use to express apologies & express politeness)

- Good morning (greeting used before 12pm)

- Good afternoon (greeting used before 5pm)

- Good night (greeting used before 12am)

- My name is []. Nice to meet you (use to introduce yourself and express gratitude)

- How do you get to []? (used to ask for directions and places to visit)

- I need help & How do I contact the police? (use if you are in trouble)

Other countries English is used in:

*Out of 195 countries, about 50 have English as a native language*

- Australia

- New Zealand

- South Africa

- Jamaica

Derived from: Germanic languages
