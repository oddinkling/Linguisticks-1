Italian

Common words and phrases:

- Hello = Buongiorno! (bwohn-johr-noh)

- My name is = Mi chiamo (mee kyah-moh)

- Yes = Sì. (see)

- No = No. (noh)

- Do you speak English? = Parla inglese? (pahr-lah een-gleh-zeh)

- Where...? = Dove? (doh-veh)

- Can i have a glass of water? = Un bicchiere di acqua (oohn bee-kyeh-reh dee ahk-wah perh fah-voh-reh)

- Please = Per favore (pehr fah-voh-reh), Per piacere (pehr pyah-cheh-reh)

- Pardon/Excuse me (formal) = Mi scusi. (mee skooh-zee)

- I'm Sorry = Mi dispiace. (mee dees-pyah-cheh)

- Scusa. (skooh-zah) (Excuse me, I’m sorry, informal)

- Thank you, No thank you = Grazie (grah-tsee-eh),No Grazie (noh grah-tsee-eh)

- Where is the bathroom? = Scusi, dov’è il bagno? (skooh-zee doh-veh eel bahn-yoh)

- How much is...? = Quanto viene? (kwahn-toh vyeh-neh)

- I am hurt = Aiuto! (ah-yooh-toh)

- I need a hospital = Dov’è l’ospedale? (doh-veh lohs-peh-dah-leh)

- Call the police = Chiamate la polizia! (chee-ah-mah-teh lah poh-lee-tsee-ah)

- Goodbye/Hello (informal) = Ciao! (chou)

- Goodbye/Hello (formal) = Arrivederci! (ahr-ree-veh-dehr-chee)

Origin country: Italy

About 64.8 million people speak Italian!
