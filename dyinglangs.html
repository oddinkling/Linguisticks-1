Parji

Origin: India

- A.K.A Duruwa, Dhurwa, Durva, Dhurwa, Parjhi, Paraja, Parajhi, Thakara, Tagara, Tugara

- Dravidian language in southeast asia

- Devanagari script & Oriya script

Mru

Origin: Bangladesh, India, Myanmar

- Aka Murung, Mro, Mrung, Maru, Niopreng, Mro-Mru, Mroo

- 50,000 native speakers worldwide

- Sino-tibetan language

- “Mru” means human and the people who speak it call themselves “Mro-cha” (human beings)

SOURCE

Ojibwe

Origin: Canada, U.S.A

- A.K.A Anishinaabemowin, Ojibwe, Ojibway, Ojibwa, Southwestern Chippewa, and Chippewa

- A Central algoquian language found in regions in Canada and some U.S border states

- Ojibwe is a dying language because the natives were forced to attend Residential Schools, which

forced them to believe their culture was “ungodly”

SOURCE

Basque

Origin: Spain, France

- Thought to be an Indo-European language (a language that predates Latin)

- Basque doesn't have any similar languages

- There are many efforts to preserve Basque, including printing books in the language
