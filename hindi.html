Hindi

Area of Origin: Southeast Asia/India

Number of Speakers: 615.4 Million

Common words and phrases:

Hindi Written
Hello my name is…
#People in India usually just say Hi or Hello. My name is….
Hindi Written: नमस्ते मेरा नाम है
English Written: namaste, mera naam hai
Do you speak English?
Hindi Written: क्या आप अंग्रेज़ी बोलते हैं
English Written: kya aap angrezee bolate hain
Yes / No
Hindi Written: हाँ, नहीैं
English Written: haaan (silent n, nasal sound) , nahee]
Directions
  1. Where is...
  2. (right, left, front, back)
  3. I lost my
  4. I am lost
  5. I am going to
  1. (Place) कहाँ है
  2. दाएँ, बाएँ, सामने, पीछे
  3. मैंने मेरा (thing) खो दिया
  4. मै खो गया हूँ
  5. मैं (place) जा रहा हूँ
  1. (Place) kahaan hai?
  2. Daye, baye, saamane, peechhe
  3. Mainne mera (object) kho diya
  4. mai kho gaya hoon
  5. main (place) ja raha hoon
  1. I am allergic to
  2. I am Vegetarian
  3. I am Vegan
  4. I am Diabetic
Hindi Written:
  1. मुझे (food) से एलर्जी है
  2. मैं एक शाकाहारी हूं
  3. मुझे डायबिटीज है
English Written
  1. mujhe (food) se elarjee hai
  2. main ek shaakaahaaree hoon
  3. mujhe daayabiteej hai
Can I have Water?
Hindi Written: क्या मुझे पानी मिलेगा
English Written: kya mujhe paanee milega
Can I order this food? (how to get food)
  1. I would like…(Food)
  2. What would you recommend?
  3. The Bill, Please
  1. मैं (food) ऑर्डर करना चाहता हूं
  2. आप क्या सलाह देते हैं
  3. कृपया बिल दीजिये
English Written
  1. main (food) ordar karana chaahata hoon
  2. aap kya salaah dete hain
  3. krpaya bil deejiye
Please People in India just use the english word "Please"
Hindi Written: कृप्या
English Written: krpya, Please
Can you repeat that please?
Hindi Written: क्या आप इसे दौहरा सकते हैं
English Written: kya aap ise dauhara sakate hain
Sorry, Excuse Me
Hindi Written: माफ़ कीजिये, मुझे माफ करें
English Written: maaf keejiye, mujhe maaph karen
Thank You/ No Thank You People in Hindi usually say "Thank you" in english.
Hindi Written: धन्यवाद/ नहीं, धन्यवाद
English Written: dhanyavaad/ nahin, dhanyavaad
Where is the Bathroom?
Hindi Written: बाथरूम कहां है
English Written: bathroom kahaan hai?
How much is this.
Hindi Written: ये कितना है
English Written: ye kitana hai
I am Hurt, I am hurt here (points to body part)
Hindi Written: मुझे चोट लगी है / मैं यहीं घायल हुआ हूं
English Written: mujhe chot lagee hai / main yaheen ghaayal hua hoon
I need a hospital/Doctor
Hindi Written: मुझे अस्पताल / डॉक्टर की आवश्यकता है
English Written: mujhe aspataal / doktar kee aavashyakata hai
Call the Police
Hindi Written: पुलिस को बुलाओ
English Written: pulis ko bulao
Help
Hindi Written: मेरी मदद करो
English Written: meree madad karo, help me.
Goodbye People in India usually just say "Bye" and other english phrases.
Hindi Written: अलविदा
English Written: alavida, bye.
